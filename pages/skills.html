Basic Hiking Skills

Hiking is one of the few things in this world that can be enjoyed by almost everyone and will work wonders for your mind, body and soul.

Plan your trip

Research, on weather conditions, travel times, routes, availability of water, dangerous terrains.

Equip yourself

Investe in high-quality boots, socks and gear. The correct equipment will keep you safe.

Check the conditions

If any adverse projections are a cause for concern, think about how you can amend your route to avoid hazards.

Leave No Trace - principles

Plan ahead, travel and camp on durable surfaces, dispose of waste properly, leave what you find, minimise campfire effects, respect wildlife and be considerate of other visitors.

Happy Hiking!
